Smart Gutters for a Modern Springfield Home

Discover how smart gutters and advanced features transformed a modern Springfield home—zero clogs, zero hassle.

The Challenge:
The Thompsons, a tech-savvy family in Galloway, wanted a gutter system that combined durability with modern convenience. Their old gutters clogged frequently, and they hated climbing ladders to check for issues.

Our Solution:

  1. Seamless Aluminum Gutters: Durable, rust-proof, and custom-fit to their roofline.
  2. Smart Gutter Guards: Micro-mesh design with built-in sensors to detect clogs and send alerts to their phone.
  3. Extended Downspouts: Redirected water 15+ feet from the foundation to prevent pooling.
  4. Rainwater Collection: Optional system to store water for gardening.

The Result:

  1. Zero Clogs: Smart guards and seamless design eliminated debris buildup.
  2. Peace of Mind: Real-time alerts mean no more ladder climbs.
  3. Eco-Friendly: Rainwater collection cut their water bill by 15%.
